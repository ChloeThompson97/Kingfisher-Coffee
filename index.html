An Inherited Family Passion

Coffee is something that many of us can't start the day without, and don't we know it! Our own infatuation with great coffee stems from our Italian lineage, where we learned that sipping an espresso in tiny streets on even tinier marble tables was, in fact, the best way to kick off the day. We use local produce to serve our clientele with sandwiches and baked goods made in-house, bringing our Italian influence to England's own tiny streets.
